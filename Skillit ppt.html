01 PROBLEM UNDERSTANDING

The Trust Deficit in
Skill Markets

Unverified Claims

Freelancers and job seekers often exaggerate skills without verification, leading to mismatched expectations and failed projects.

No Skill Barter System

Traditional platforms force monetary transactions. There's no way to exchange skills directly — "I'll build your website, you design my logo."

Recruiter Inefficiency

Recruiters spend countless hours filtering unverified profiles. No unified platform ranks talent based on actual verified abilities.

Monetary Barriers

Premium features are locked behind expensive subscriptions. Talent from tier-2/3 cities can't afford premium job platform access.
